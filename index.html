Starfield INI Tweaks

Starfield INI’s ca be edited directly in MO2, Tools->Tool Plugins -> INI Editor

Starfield.ini

[Launcher]
bEnableFileSelection=1

StarfieldPrefs.ini

[Workshop]
fItemRotationSpeed=1.5

StarfieldCustom.ini

[Archive]
bInvalidateOlderFiles=1
sResourceDataDirsFinal=

[Camera]
fFPWorldFOV=95
fTPWorldFOV=95
